# Writing Knowledge Workflow

The writing workflow was designed for formal Chinese office materials, including:

- Company communications.
- Training reports.
- Visit reports.
- Work plans and proposals.
- Meeting summaries and internal briefs.

## Knowledge Base Structure

```text
writing-kb/
├── 01-source-dossiers/
├── 04-document-patterns/
├── 05-best-outputs/
├── 06-templates-and-constraints/
└── 07-house-style-and-voice/
```

## Workflow

1. Collect source materials: notices, schedules, screenshots, PDFs, DOCX files, and prior drafts.
2. Extract reusable patterns: title formulas, lead structures, subheading rhythm, ending style, and formal expression.
3. Draft with constraints: use confirmed facts only, avoid invented names and numbers, keep tone formal and steady.
4. Export deliverables: preferably DOCX for review and archival.
5. Promote final drafts back into the knowledge base as best outputs.

## Key Lesson

Prompting alone is not enough for professional writing. The Agent needs a managed memory system: examples, style observations, constraints, and final outputs.
